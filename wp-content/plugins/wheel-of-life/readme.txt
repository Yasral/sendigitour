== Key Features ==

* **Unlimited Assessments** - You can easily create unlimited assessments for your clients. Your clients can take the assessment multiple times to analyze how balanced their life is. This even encourages them to sign up for coaching services from you to create a fulfilling life.

* **Customization Options** - Wheel of Life is simple and easy to use. You can fully customize the different settings of the assessment tool, such as the visual appearance of the wheel, choices in color options and many more.

* **Call to Action Integration** - To encourage your clients and your website visitors to signup for your services or book an appointment, you can add Call to Action at the end of the assessment. This will help you bring more leads and grow your coaching business.

* **Easily Embed Across Your Website** - You can easily embed the assessment across your website in posts or pages using shortcode and button blocks.

* **Social Sharing** - Your clients and website visitors can quickly share their assessment results on different social media. This will help to increase social media visibility about your business and generate leads.

* **Email Report** - At the end of the assessment, your clients and website visitors can result of the rest to their email their results for future reference.

* **Compatible with Any Theme** - This plugin works smoothly with any theme.

== Wheel of Life Pro Features ==

* **Analytics Dashboard** -  With the Pro version, you can get how your clients and website visitors are engaged with the assessments. You can see the total number of assessments taken, the popular assessments and who recently took the assessment.

* **User Registration** - With this feature, you can allow your clients and website visitors to register on your website to take assessments from their profile, view their assessment history and much more. You can also promote helpful resources on the dashboard.

* **More Insights on Members** - You can see the progress of your registered users from your dashboard. You can easily review your clients‚Äô profiles, see their assessment history and much more.

* **Customizable Email Templates** - You can customize the email templates that are sent during registration, forgot password and email report.

* **Resources Sharing** - You can add several helpful resources to inform your clients and generate more leads. The helpful resources are displayed on the user dashboard.

* **More Chart Types** - You get 3 different chart types for displaying the assessment which can be configured independently for each assessment.

* **Custom Call to Action** - In addition to the Global Call to Action, you can configure different CTA for each of your assessments. This feature will offer more control to customize the CTA for each assessment and will help you bring more leads.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who is the Wheel of Life plugin for? =

Wheel of Life plugin is for coaches who want to let their website visitors take an assessment, generate leads and grow their coaching business. It is also for the coaches who want to let their clients get a ‚Äúbird-eye‚Äù view about their life and help them to achieve profound balance.

= How do I use the Wheel of Life? =

The Wheel of Life assessment plugin is easy to configure and simple to use. We have [step-by-step](https://kraftplugins.com/wheel-of-life/docs/) tutorials on how you can use it. Once you create, publish and add the assessment you have created, your users can take the assessment.

= How long does it take to take the assessment? =

It usually takes less than a minute to complete the assessment. If you have added many areas of life to rate, it might take some more time than usual.

= Does Wheel of Life work with any theme? =

Yes, the plugin can be used with any theme of your choice. It will work smoothly without any issues.
